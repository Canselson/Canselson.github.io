Privacy Policy

Southampton Spitfires — southamptonspitfires.me — Effective 6 May 2026

This privacy policy applies between you, the User of this Website, and Southampton Spitfires, the owner and provider of this Website. Southampton Spitfires takes the privacy of your information very seriously. This privacy policy applies to our use of any and all Data collected by us or provided by you in relation to your use of the Website.

Please read this privacy policy carefully.

2. Scope of This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies only to the actions of Southampton Spitfires and Users with respect to this Website. It does not extend to any websites that can be accessed from this Website, including links to social media websites.

For the purposes of the applicable Data Protection Laws, Southampton Spitfires is the "data controller" — meaning we determine the purposes for which, and the manner in which, your Data is processed.

3. Data Collected

We may collect the following Data from you when you submit the contact/membership enquiry form on this Website:

  • Name;
  • Mobile telephone number;
  • University of study;
  • Skating skill level (beginner, intermediate, or advanced); and
  • Any message or enquiry you choose to include.

4. How We Collect Data

We collect Data only when it is given to us directly by you — for example, when you contact us through the enquiry form on the Website or by any other means such as telephone or email.

5. Our Use of Data

Data collected by us may be used for the following purposes:

  • Responding to your membership enquiry;
  • Internal record keeping; and
  • Communicating information about the club and its activities.

We may use your Data for the above purposes where we deem it necessary for our legitimate interests. If you are not satisfied with this, you have the right to object in certain circumstances (see the section headed "Your Rights" below).

6. Who We Share Data With

We may share your Data with the following:

  • Southampton Spitfires committee members and administrators — to process your enquiry and ensure sessions are organised accurately.

We do not sell your Data to third parties or share it with any other external organisations.

7. Keeping Data Secure

We will use technical and organisational measures to safeguard your Data. Your Data is stored on secure servers provided by our data hosting partner, Supabase Inc., which employs industry-standard security practices.

If you suspect any misuse, loss, or unauthorised access to your Data, please contact us immediately at suiihc@soton.ac.uk.

8. Data Retention

Unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law, we will only hold your Data for the period necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this privacy policy, or until you request that the Data be deleted.

Even if we delete your Data, it may persist on backup or archival media for legal, tax, or regulatory purposes.

9. Your Rights

You have the following rights in relation to your Data:

  • Right to access — the right to request copies of the information we hold about you.
  • Right to correct — the right to have your Data rectified if it is inaccurate or incomplete.
  • Right to erase — the right to request that we delete or remove your Data from our systems.
  • Right to restrict our use of your Data — the right to limit the way in which we can use your Data.
  • Right to data portability — the right to request that we move, copy, or transfer your Data.
  • Right to object — the right to object to our use of your Data, including where we use it for our legitimate interests.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at suiihc@soton.ac.uk.

If you are not satisfied with how we handle a complaint, you may refer it to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at https://ico.org.uk/.

10. Links to Other Websites

This Website may provide links to other websites, including social media platforms. We have no control over such websites and are not responsible for their content. This privacy policy does not extend to your use of those websites, and you are advised to read their respective privacy policies before using them.

11. Changes of Business Ownership and Control

Southampton Spitfires may from time to time expand or reduce its operations, and this may involve the transfer of control of all or part of Southampton Spitfires. Data provided by Users will, where relevant, be transferred along with that part of the business, and the new controlling party will be permitted to use the Data only for the purposes for which it was originally supplied under the terms of this privacy policy.

12. General

You may not transfer any of your rights under this privacy policy to any other person. If any provision of this privacy policy is found to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions will not be affected. This privacy policy will be governed by and interpreted according to the law of England and Wales, and all disputes will be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the English and Welsh courts.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Southampton Spitfires reserves the right to change this privacy policy as deemed necessary or as required by law. Any changes will be posted on the Website and, where documents have been distributed, an updated version will be made available. Continued use of the Website following any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

You may contact Southampton Spitfires by email at suiihc@soton.ac.uk.
